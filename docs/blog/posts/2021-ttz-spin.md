# Predicting ttZ spin correlations at the LHC

My paper on spin correlations in $t\bar{t}Z$ production has been published in the [European Physical Journal C](https://doi.org/10.1140/epjc/s10052-021-09605-8). This phenomenological study laid the groundwork for the first experimental measurement of this quantum effect.

<!-- more -->

In this paper, we computed the full spin density matrix for $t\bar{t}Z$ production at NLO in QCD and found that the additional $Z$ boson emission leads to significantly different spin correlations compared to the more familiar $t\bar{t}$ case.

We also showed that the angular information in the spin density matrix provides novel constraints on dimension-6 EFT operators — constraints that are independent of the total production rate.

The experimental measurement followed two years later in our legacy ttZ paper, confirming the prediction.
